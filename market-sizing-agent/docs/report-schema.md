# Market Sizing Report Schema

## Output Format

Reports are saved as `.md` files to `./reports/{company-slug}-market-sizing-{timestamp}.md`.

---

## Report Sections

### Header

```markdown
# Market Sizing Report: Acme HRTech
*Prepared for Priya Krishnan · priya@acme-hrtech.com · Generated 2024-01-15*
```

---

### Executive Summary

3–4 sentence summary covering:
- TAM / SAM / SOM headline numbers
- Data confidence level
- #1 actionable recommendation

---

### Market Overview

- Category description
- Top tailwinds (why now)
- CAGR and forecast period with source citation

---

### TAM — Total Addressable Market

```markdown
## TAM — Total Addressable Market

Your TAM is estimated at **$14.2B** globally for AI-powered HR performance 
management software [1].

**Methodology:** Bottom-up calculation based on 280M knowledge workers globally 
× $50 average per-seat annual spend for HR performance tools. Cross-validated 
against IDC's 2024 HCM market report projecting $18B by 2027 at 11.2% CAGR.

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Global market size | $14.2B |
| CAGR (2024–2029) | 11.2% |
| 2029 forecast | $24.1B |
```

---

### SAM — Serviceable Addressable Market

Filters TAM by:
- Geography (target location)
- Buyer segment (primary buyer + company size implied by ACV)

```markdown
## SAM — Serviceable Addressable Market

**Your SAM is $2.8B** — the US share of the HR performance management market 
addressable by mid-market companies with VP People / CHRO buyers [2][3].

**Geography filter:** United States = ~25% of global TAM ($3.6B)  
**Segment filter:** Mid-market segment (200–2000 employees) = ~78% of US market
```

---

### SOM — Serviceable Obtainable Market

Applies stage + ACV + GTM motion multiplier:

```markdown
## SOM — Serviceable Obtainable Market

**Your 3-year SOM target is $56M** (2% of SAM), consistent with Series A 
companies achieving product-market fit in this segment.

| Year | Target ARR | % of SAM |
|---|---|---|
| Year 1 | $2.8M | 0.1% |
| Year 2 | $14M | 0.5% |
| Year 3 | $56M | 2.0% |
```
