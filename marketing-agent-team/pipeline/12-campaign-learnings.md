### Campaign: Early Customer Persona vs ICP — B2B GTM Clarity Campaign — 2026-03-17 (COMPLETE)

**Phase completed**: Full campaign — Phases 1–4 complete. All content written, QA passed, scheduled.
**Phase 1 summary** (Strategy + Research): content-strategist, market-researcher, audience-analyst, social-listener
**Deliverables planned**: LinkedIn post (3 variants), cold email sequence (3 emails), blog post
**Agents run**: content-strategist, market-researcher, audience-analyst, social-listener

**Research quality notes**:
- market-researcher found a near-exact ECP framework from GTM Strategist Substack ("Before there is Ideal, there is Early") — content team must differentiate by being more accessible, hook-forward, and ARR-milestone anchored
- The ECP acronym is not yet mainstream — this campaign has a genuine first-mover opportunity to define and own the term
- audience-analyst confirmed two distinct pain modes: Founders (survival/revenue pain) vs PMMs (credibility/influence pain). Hooks should target Founders first; PMMs self-select.
- social-listener confirmed: LinkedIn's 2026 Depth Score weights comment quality heavily. Do NOT use generic engagement bait ("tag a founder"). Close with a specific question instead.

**Strategic decisions logged**:
- Primary angle: "The $1M ARR persona is not the $10M ARR persona" — stakes-first, consequence-led
- Minimum viable content set: LinkedIn (3 variants) + Blog. Email sequence is high-value but can be dropped if bandwidth constrained.
- AI-acceleration context is supporting color only — do NOT make it the headline
- Blog SEO opportunity: "early customer persona B2B GTM" is a low-competition keyword cluster — first-mover ranking opportunity

**Metrics to track after publish**:
- LinkedIn: Which hook format (contrarian / stakes / authority) drives highest engagement rate
- Email: Which subject line pattern achieves >35% open rate; which email in 3-part sequence gets most replies
- Blog: Time-on-page, scroll depth, keyword ranking at 30 and 60 days
- Cross-channel: Do LinkedIn engagers convert to blog sessions? (use UTM links in first comment)

### Campaign: Early Customer Persona vs ICP — Phase 2–4 Run — 2026-03-17

**Agents run**: linkedin-writer, email-writer, blog-writer, qa-agent, seo-optimizer, brand-voice-checker, ab-test-designer, content-calendar-planner

**Content produced**:
- pipeline/04-linkedin-draft.md — 3 LinkedIn post variants (Mistake Hook, Contrarian Reframe, Stakes/ARR Hook)
- pipeline/05-email-draft.md — 3-email cold outreach sequence
- pipeline/06-blog-draft.md — ~2,050 word SEO blog post

**Content angles used and rationale**:
- Mistake Hook (Variant A): "Most founders build GTM for a customer who doesn't exist yet" — leads with failure state, highest predicted comment rate, most accessible to problem-unaware audience
- Contrarian Reframe (Variant B): "Your ICP isn't the problem" — ICP-familiar audience, predicted highest save rate, PMM crossover appeal
- Stakes/ARR Hook (Variant C): "$1M ARR persona will stall you at $3M" — financial anchoring, predicted highest reshare + co-founder tag rate
- Email 1 subject line winner (recommended): "Why your outbound isn't converting (it's not the copy)" — names acute pain + pattern interrupt
- Blog angle: Led with founder fear ("Am I building for a customer I wish existed?"), then ECP definition, ECP vs ICP table, 4-step building framework, downstream impact, summary

**Quality team findings**:
- QA: PASS — 0 Critical, 3 Major (all fixable), 5 Minor. No revision loop required.
  - Key Major: GTM Strategist attribution needs hyperlink or rephrase in blog; Email 2 subject line C has framing mismatch
  - Brand voice: PASS — 3 minor fixes applied (LinkedIn Variant C copy, Email 2 structure, blog ICP description)
  - SEO: PASS WITH OPTIMIZATIONS — 7 priority actions logged, 4 applied pre-publish, 3 are publish-day tasks
- No Critical issues found across any file — content cleared for scheduling without a revision loop

**Key decisions this run**:
- "Early adopter persona SaaS" secondary keyword not present in draft — SEO optimizer flagged; recommend adding in future blog revisions or follow-up posts
- Blog does not use the "68% higher win rate" or Gartner stats — correctly avoided per market researcher's verification caveat
- GTM Strategist ECP framework referenced by name in blog — must add hyperlink on publish or rephrase to avoid unattributed attribution
- Email sequence uses no hard CTA — educate-first structure maintained across all 3 emails; CTA is follow/connect only

**Recommended optimizations for next campaign**:
1. Test the authority observation hook ("After seeing 50+ early-stage GTM motions...") as a 4th LinkedIn variant — not yet tested but social-listener flagged it as high comment-quality driver
2. Consider writing a follow-up blog post: "How to Use Your Early Customer Persona to Write Cold Emails" — natural SEO cluster and internal link target
3. If LinkedIn Variant C (ARR hook) drives the highest reshare rate as predicted, use ARR-milestone framing as the default opener for future B2B GTM posts
4. Validate the audience quotes in pipeline/02-audience-insights.md with real founder interviews before next campaign — currently knowledge-based estimates
5. Add UTM parameters to the blog link dropped in LinkedIn first comments — enables cross-channel conversion tracking (LinkedIn engager → blog session)
